Web Development

Master the art of building modern, responsive, and interactive websites

What You'll Learn

Web development is the foundation of the digital world. This course covers everything from basic HTML to advanced frameworks. You will learn how to design, develop, and deploy websites and web applications that are visually stunning and fully functional.

  • HTML5 & CSS3 fundamentals
  • Responsive design with Bootstrap
  • JavaScript programming & DOM manipulation
  • jQuery and animations
  • Building projects using React.js
  • Hosting and deploying your site

Who Should Take This Course?

This course is ideal for beginners, college students, aspiring front-end developers, or anyone interested in launching a career in tech. No prior coding experience is needed — just curiosity and motivation.

Benefits of Learning Web Development

  • High demand skill with excellent job prospects
  • Ability to freelance or work remotely
  • Build your own projects, portfolio, and websites
  • Prepares you for internships, hackathons, and startups
